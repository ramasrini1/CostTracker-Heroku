The CostTracker app computes and splits your travel/restaurant expenses among friends

Have you gone on a trip with your friends and wondered how to split the cost of the total travel expenses? The Cost Tracker app will help you split the cost and make the payments to the individual bank accounts.

This web app will provide an interface for each individual in the group to enter their cost and (Event)trip name. An algorithm will be used to determine the amount that each individual will contribute. And a payment request or money will be sent to each userâ€™s venmo account using the venmo api through the Cost Tracker App. A user gets money deposited or gets a request payment depending upon their contribution to the trip.

The money transaction as of now is only handled by the admin. The users of the app just need to enter their expenses for the trip and their expenses will be settled by the admin.

User Flow

  • Sign Up/Login
  • Add Event: Creates A New Event
  • Add Expense: Select Event->Add Friend ->Enter Expense ->Submit Expense
  • Split Expense -> View Payment/Expense Report
  • Admin Settles the Balance
Check the Demo Account for user experience

Tools And Technology

  • Python Flask, Jinja Template
  • Database: Postgresql
  • CSS/HTML
  • Venmo SDK
